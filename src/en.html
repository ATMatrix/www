Build a Next-Generation Blockchain with AIaaS

Atmatrix aims to provide a next-generation blockchain with smart-contract platform and AIaaS. It will make all DApps have good opportunity to get AI capability and service. Atmatrix will make the DBots/DApps ecosystem grow prosperously.

Build a Blockchain-based World AI

Atmatrix aims to provide a consensus based distributed virtual AI-as-a-Service cloud. With the aid of blockchain economic system, Atmatrix coordinates global AI technologies to build a new world of artificial intelligence.

FEATURES

Connect the Blockchain World and the AI World

The first step in sharing AI is how we build a service that connects different AI services through Blockchain and how to build a bridge between the Blockchain smart contract world and the AI world for user benefit.

Atmatrix's second step is to get more AI and smart contract linked to the service, rather than part of the participants or platform in the center, including more open and unlimited access to AI, and more blockchain network so the off-chain platform in the first phase will evolve into a stand-alone blockchain platform, token contract in the first phase will transfer to the independent main blockchain and become to change as the main ATT token. This independent main blockchain network, will include all the different DBot nodes.

Trustless AI Interoperability

Compared with the traditional AI Service, the AI system in Atmatrix is more easy to deploy. Join AtmatrixNetwork is very simple:

  • Packaging the existing AI Service as API and Schema provided by Atmatrix
  • Develop DBot Oracle program, access AI Service, and deployed to Atmatrix multiple Oracle Relay Server.
  • Develop agents that call AI service smart contract, define prices and other parameters, and deploy and register to Atmatrix's AI Service management smart contract.

Open Platform

ATMaxtrix is an Open Platform that can be extended with Smart Contract to implement interaction and collaboration with other Ethereum-based DApps.

ATMaxtrix's open source features enable third-party developers to build transactionable applications better on Platform. The Platform can support multiple applications.

USE CASES

Decentralized Robots (DBots)

Decentralization is one of the characteristics of the Atmatrix blockchain. The Atmatrix blockchain develops different modules for different systems and users to simplify the preparation of development and to facilitate the DBots and DApps development.

With the introduction of incentive mechanisms and sharing economy, the Atmatrix blockchain will change the current application market and business model. The Atmatrix DBots and DApps marketplace can include decentralized social network, storage, DNS, and computing services.

Industry Oriented

Atmatrix provides support for business development by using industry focused consensus mechanism and incorporating regulatory requirements.

Atmatrix also can support many industry application requirements, such as Finance, Logistics, Supply chain, Social and gaming, Charity, Digital assets, Stocks, etc.

Smart Contract Control of AI as a Service

For example, an insurance-related contract needs to call the AI to obtain the weather conditions (temperature, disaster probability) of Shanghai next month to help the insurance contract complete the weather-related odds in the region, and follow-up insurance Will be automatically executed according to this odds. Because the Smart Contract is deterministic in every node in a network such as Ethereum, any deterministic errors can result in the failure of the Network Consensus, so that the deterministic Smart Contracts executed by the nodes can not invoke the external service directly. They will receive external AI information and data by performing a collection of information from the Account elected by Smart Contract on the chain and performing the Off-chain Consensus process. Smart Contract will have the high timeliness and reliability of external information due to the bridge provided by Atmatrix to AI Service.

AI-Based Smart Contract Decentralized Governance

Aragon Network proposed a Decentralized judicial arbitration mechanism based on Smart Contract. This case will be further refined on this basis to provide more efficient, efficient and fair and transparent support to Judent in Decentralized Judicial Arbitration. From the two aspects, the use of AI more efficient fact data and evidence to obtain, the use of Off-chain Consensus in the AI ​​data on the basis of more transparent conclusion of the arbitration. AI can completely replace the human may also be controversial, but because the process of human decision making in the black box of the brain, there are a lot of uncertainty and not credible, AI reason they are good at learning and Blockchain Deterministic areas are better than humans, and future decisions by the Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAO) will most likely be replaced by AI, but before that, Atmatrix's DBot Account can still retain the "judge" role, with classes Aragon Of the system remains compatible, but the judge will be able to be replaced by AI.

Blockchain Network emphasizes certainty, certainty and trust and low risk. AI replacement arbitration judges will bring certainty to enhance the network's trust and reduce system risk.

AI Service Interoperability

Atmatrix hopes to provide interoperability between AI Service for this future Atmatrix. When you need to complete a complex AI task, through the operation of other AI Service to work together to complete the most economical and feasible way. In Atmatrix, AI Service interoperability is through Smart Contract and DBot Platform to implement, Register in AtmatrixRegister table Smart Contract in the AI Service has been standardized, other AI Service call only pay a certain fee, can be called The Because Atmatrix is open, without mutual trust and without authorization, AtmatrixNetwork can also be understood as the Network infrastructure and value switching network between the AI Service Provider and User.
